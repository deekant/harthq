Plain English summary: We collect your name, email, and practice details when you use our tools or purchase our products. We use this to deliver our services and, if you opt in, to send you relevant practice growth content. We don't sell your data. You can access, correct, or delete your information at any time by contacting us.

1. About us

HartHQ (ABN 46 143 297 509, ACN 143 297 509) is an Australian business providing practice growth and administrative support services to psychologists, counsellors, and therapists in private practice. HartHQ operates as part of The Hart Centre group.

This Privacy Policy applies to all products and services offered through harthq.com.au, including the HartBeat Score tool, Practice Toolkits, Annual Reviews, and Concierge services.

By using our website or purchasing our products, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. Information we collect

We collect personal information only when it is reasonably necessary for our business functions. The types of information we may collect include:

Information you provide directly

  • Name and email address (via the HartBeat Score tool, lead capture forms, or account creation)
  • Practice details such as session fee, caseload size, and hours worked (via the HartBeat Score tool) - this information is used to generate your personalised score and recommendations
  • Payment information when purchasing products or services (processed securely via our payment provider; we do not store card details)
  • Any information you provide when contacting us directly by email or booking a call

Information collected automatically

  • Website usage data such as pages visited, time on site, and referring URLs (via analytics tools)
  • Browser type, device type, and approximate location (country/state level only)
  • Cookie data as described in Section 8 below

Sensitive information

We do not intentionally collect sensitive information as defined under the Privacy Act (such as health information, religious beliefs, or racial background). Our tools relate to business and financial information about your practice, not personal clinical information.

3. How we use your information

We use the personal information we collect to:

  • Deliver the products and services you have purchased or requested
  • Generate your HartBeat Score and practice recommendations
  • Process payments and issue invoices or receipts
  • Send transactional emails related to your purchase or enquiry (e.g. order confirmations, booking confirmations)
  • Send practice growth content, product updates, and other marketing communications - only if you have opted in
  • Improve our website and services through aggregated, non-identifying analytics
  • Respond to your enquiries and provide customer support
  • Meet our legal and regulatory obligations

We will not use your information for any purpose that is incompatible with the purpose for which it was collected, without your consent.

4. Third-party services

We use a small number of trusted third-party services to operate our business. Each is selected for its security practices and compliance with applicable privacy laws. We share only the minimum information necessary for these services to function.

Service Purpose What is shared
Klaviyo Email marketing and automation Name, email address, and any practice details you provide via opt-in forms. Only shared if you have opted in to receive communications.
Stripe Payment processing Name, email, and billing information required to complete a purchase. Card details are handled entirely by Stripe and are never stored by us.
Zoho Bookings Appointment scheduling Name and email when you book a call through our booking link.
WordPress.org / hosting provider Website infrastructure General site traffic data. No personal information is stored by the hosting platform beyond standard server logs.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for their own marketing purposes.

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, or in response to a valid request from a government or law enforcement authority.

5. Marketing emails

We send marketing communications (such as practice tips, product updates, and offers) only to people who have explicitly opted in to receive them - for example, by submitting their email address via the HartBeat Score tool or another opt-in form on our website.

Every marketing email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You can opt out at any time by clicking that link or by emailing us at hq@thehartcentre.com.au. We will process your request promptly and you will receive no further marketing communications.

Opting out of marketing emails does not affect transactional emails related to your account or purchases.

6. Storage and security

We take reasonable steps to protect the personal information we hold from misuse, interference, loss, and unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure.

These measures include password protection, access controls, and use of encrypted connections (HTTPS) across our website.

While we take all reasonable precautions, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of information transmitted to or from our website.

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by law. When information is no longer needed, we take reasonable steps to destroy or de-identify it.

7. Access and correction

Under the Australian Privacy Act, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request that we correct any personal information that is inaccurate, out of date, incomplete, or misleading
  • Request that we delete your personal information (subject to any legal obligations we have to retain it)

To make any of these requests, please contact us at hq@thehartcentre.com.au. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe - generally within 30 days. In some cases, we may need to verify your identity before we can action your request.

There is no charge for making a request, though we may charge a reasonable fee if responding requires significant effort.

8. Cookies

Our website uses cookies - small text files stored on your device - to support website functionality and improve your experience. Cookies we may use include:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function correctly (e.g. maintaining your session during checkout). These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it. These collect aggregated, non-identifying data.
  • Marketing cookies: May be set by third-party services such as Klaviyo to track email engagement and serve relevant content. These are only active if you have opted in to marketing communications.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain parts of our website.

9. External links

Our website may contain links to external websites, including The Hart Centre (thehartcentre.com.au) and third-party service providers. This Privacy Policy applies only to harthq.com.au. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any linked external sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Complaints

If you have a concern about how we have handled your personal information, please contact us first at hq@thehartcentre.com.au. We take all privacy complaints seriously and will respond within 30 days.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC):

  • Website: www.oaic.gov.au
  • Phone: 1300 363 992
